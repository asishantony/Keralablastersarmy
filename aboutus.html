WHO WE ARE

Kerala Blasters Army is the registered fan club of KERALA BLASTERS FC with Registration No: EKM/TC/138/17. The club consists of fans from all the districts of Kerala and also from abroad. The membership of the club is given with strict guidelines. Each member should give their Name, Full contact address, Phone Number, Identity card copy (any one approved by Government of India or State Government of Kerala). They also need to agree to the rules and regulations of the club. Kerala blasters army is conducting various activities in and out of the stadium to nurture a football culture in Kerala and to support Kerala Blasters FC

MISSION

Kerala Blasters army is a mission driven Fans club aiming at improving the quality of Football in Kerala. It also aims to spread awareness among people for the need of a football culture.

VISION

Kerala Blasters Army’s vision is to provide a foot ball friendly ecosystem for the younger footballers and to the Generations to come by supporting Kerala Blasters FC.

HISTORY

Indian Super League (ISL) paved a new era of football viewership in India. From the 5000 or so average attendance in the I League to a blistering 27000 average attendance of ISL took players and clubs to the lime light. The Kerala football lovers were delighted hearing the announcement of the Kerala based foot ball club KERALA BLASTERS FC. A group of football lovers formed a group supporting Kerala blasters FC and they named it KERALA BLASTERS ARMY. At first the members of the group were interacting through Social media. But in matter of months the group became a family. They realised that their activities should not be limited to Social media. So they decided to come together to form a fans club. Although the members were scattered all over Kerala and unknown personally to one another, they came together at a members’ house to form the club in 2015. The first meeting was attended by 27 members from different districts of Kerala. The members discussed and formulated the club mission and vision. The first committee was formed and decided to form the fan club as a formal club with registration. The main mission of the club was to strengthen football in Kerala by supporting the Kerala Blasters FC. After the all Kerala meeting, we arranged meetings at different districts and formed district wings. Also the club was officially registered according to Societies registration act 1860 with the Kerala registration department. The club survived different crises on the path to become the first Registered Fan club of Kerala Blasters FC. While the members of social media groups were increasing, the club was discussing about the activities to carry out for the development of football in Kerala. The main challenge faced by the club which consisted of common members was financial crisis. Besides all this crises the club was able to organise different programs in and out of the stadium like conducting free foot ball coaching camps, distribution of free kits for young footballers and Charity works. The club is organising different programs during ISL for Kerala Blasters F C like Match day Rally, Decorations in the West Gallery etc. The club rejuvenated the West Gallery by amalgamating other small and scattered fan groups of Kerala Blasters FC. The members, who are minimum wage workers, donated a section of their hard earned money to set things in the right direction. Today the club consists of 150 Registered Members, an All Kerala committee, District Committees, more than 40 active WhatsApp groups, a Telegram group with 4000 members, Telegram channel, and an active Face book group.
